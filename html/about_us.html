About Us

Our Story

Sprout & Grow is a greenhouse nursery in Raleigh, NC, specializing in high-quality organic plants. The nursery aims to provide customers with a diverse selection of meticulously nurtured plants that promote sustainable gardening practices.

Team

Sprout & Grow prioritizes transparency, integrity, and environmental stewardship, offering a range of educational resources and personalized support to empower customers in cultivating thriving organic gardens.
